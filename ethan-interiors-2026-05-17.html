Our Philosophy

Design Should Serve
How You Live

Ethan Interiors was founded by Ethan Goh, who spent years at two of Singapore's largest ID firms before choosing to build something more intentional. A boutique studio where every home is designed from scratch — not templated, not rushed.

Our team of interior design and architectural graduates considers colour, proportion, the interplay of light and shadow, and the golden ratio — because the homes we make must feel as good as they look.

"A home should do more than look good — it should enhance and support the lives of its inhabitants, so as to allow them to do their best in life."
— Ethan Goh, Founder

How We Work

A Process Built Around
Clarity & Craft

01
Design Consultation

We begin by understanding how you live — your routines, priorities, and the way you want your home to feel. This forms the foundation for every decision that follows.

02
Design & Development

From spatial planning to material selection, every element is resolved with intent. Refined renders help you see the space before a single tool is lifted.

03
Project Execution

Our dedicated project management team, personally trained by Ethan, oversees every build with the same precision and quality standard applied to every home.

04
Handover & Beyond

We don't disappear after handover. Our trusted contractors — partners since 2020 — stand behind every detail to ensure your home stands the test of time.
