Our Story

A CUT
ABOVE THE
HEARTLAND

Limpeh Barbershop was born in 2015 from a bold vision: Siti Rafidah — known as Fidd — opened her doors in the quiet streets of Bedok North, choosing to serve the community she grew up in.

The name "Limpeh" means my father in Hokkien, and the tagline says it all: "Hensem like your limpeh" — because every man who walks out of this shop should feel that kind of confidence.

What started as a one-woman show is now a four-barber team — Johan, Asyraf, Zack, and Fidd — delivering precision cuts and genuine warmth, appointment by appointment, cut by cut.
