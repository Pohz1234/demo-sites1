Who We Are

Interior Design
with Soul & Purpose

We're a Singapore-based interior design studio specialising in Japandi, Wabi-Sabi, and Scandinavian-inspired homes. Our team — Willy, Kok Leong, Alan, and Jeffery — brings warmth and precision to every project, from BTO flats in Tampines to resale condos in the city fringe.

We are HDB Licensed, bizSAFE certified, and members of the Renovation Contractors & Material Suppliers Association (RCMA). Every home we build is managed in-house — no subcontracting, no surprises.

How We Work

Your Renovation Journey,
Start to Finish

01
Consult & Discover

Share your floor plan, inspiration images, and budget. We listen first — no pressure, no templates. Just honest conversation about what your home could be.

02
Design & Visualise

Our team develops space plans, mood boards, and 3D visuals tailored to your goals. You'll see your home before a single nail is hammered.

03
Refine & Confirm

We walk through every detail with you — layout tweaks, material selections, fittings — until you're 100% confident in the direction.

04
Build & Deliver

Our in-house team handles all works with regular photo updates and quality checks. We don't hand over until it's right.
