Our Story

Rooted in
Clementi
Since 2018

The Men's Mane was born from a simple conviction: every man deserves a cut that feels intentional. We set up shop at Blk 354 Clementi Ave 2 with sharp blades, steady hands, and an uncompromising commitment to the craft.

"A great barber doesn't just cut hair — he shapes how you carry yourself into the world."

Seven years in, that philosophy hasn't changed. Our barbers stay sharp — studying technique, listening to every client who sits in the chair. Whether you're a regular from the block or dropping in for the first time, you'll leave looking better than when you walked in.
